# ARCHITECTURE.md

Project Name:
Logic Loving Mind

Target Industry:
Real Estate

Primary Audience:

* Real Estate Developers
* Real Estate Brokers
* Property Consultants
* Project Sales Teams

Primary Pain:

Missed leads.

Missed follow-ups.

Missed site visits.

Lost commissions.

Manual operations.

Business dependent on owner.

---

TECH STACK

Mandatory:

* Next.js
* TypeScript
* React Three Fiber
* Three.js
* GSAP
* ScrollTrigger
* Framer Motion
* GLSL Shaders
* Post Processing
* Lenis Smooth Scroll

Use GPU accelerated rendering whenever possible.

Keep a persistent 3D world mounted.

Do not destroy and recreate scenes.

The world should evolve continuously.

---

VISUAL WORLD

The website is one continuous cinematic environment.

No section transitions.

No page transitions.

No obvious boundaries.

Everything exists inside a single world.

---

SCENE 1

THE LEAD STORM

Thousands of inquiries floating through space.

Examples:

* Site Visit Request
* Interested
* Price?
* Available?
* Call Me
* 3 BHK?
* Possession Date?

Use depth.

Use particles.

Use volumetrics.

User flies through them.

The experience should feel overwhelming.

---

SCENE 2

THE REVENUE LEAK

Lead cards begin disappearing.

Commission values fade away.

Examples:

₹1.5L

₹3L

₹5L

₹8L

Lost opportunities drift into darkness.

The environment becomes more chaotic.

---

SCENE 3

THE AI CORE

A distant gold intelligence sphere appears.

Not a robot.

Not a chatbot.

A mysterious intelligence.

Messages begin curving toward it.

Everything is attracted to it.

Use shader effects.

Use volumetric glow.

Use particle attraction systems.

---

SCENE 4

THE TRANSFORMATION

Messages enter the core.

Outputs emerge.

Qualified Lead

Booked Site Visit

Follow-Up Sent

CRM Updated

Appointment Confirmed

Environment becomes organized.

Motion becomes calm.

---

SCENE 5

THE SCALE ENGINE

Massive data highways.

Property pipelines.

Automation networks.

Lead flows.

Booking flows.

Reporting flows.

Everything synchronized.

The environment expands dramatically.

The user should feel freedom.

---

SCENE 6

THE SKYLINE

A luxury city emerges from organized data.

Buildings generated from light streams.

The city represents scalable growth.

The AI core remains central.

---

SHADER REQUIREMENTS

Use custom GLSL extensively.

Include:

* volumetric fog
* depth haze
* bloom
* chromatic aberration
* motion trails
* light scattering
* particle distortion
* procedural noise
* cinematic color grading

No template visuals.

No default Three.js look.

Everything custom.

---

QUALITY RULES

Every element must appear premium.

Every particle must have purpose.

Every motion must feel intentional.

Every transition must feel seamless.

No abrupt state changes.

No cheap effects.

No generic UI.

No obvious templates.

The final result should feel like an interactive luxury film for real-estate automation.

Target reaction:

"How much did they spend on this website?"
